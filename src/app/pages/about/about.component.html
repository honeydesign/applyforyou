ApplyForYou puts the power of AI on your side. We tailor your CV to every job, apply the moment roles are posted, and make sure you're always first in line.

Our story

It started with a friend.

A close friend was struggling to find a job. Not because they weren't talented — they absolutely were. But sending application after application, tailoring CVs by hand, waiting weeks to hear nothing back — it was draining them.

"I just want someone to apply for me while I focus on preparing for interviews."

That one sentence was the spark. We built ApplyForYou to do exactly that — take over the repetitive, time-consuming part of job hunting so that talented people can focus on what actually matters: showing up prepared and confident when the interview comes.

What started as a tool for one friend quickly became something more. Because that friend's struggle wasn't unique — it was shared by thousands of ambitious Africans trying to break through in a system that wasn't designed with them in mind.

Today, ApplyForYou is that friend for everyone.
